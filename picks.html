Jerry says Cowboys QB Prescott (hamstring) 'likely' headed to Injury Reserve (IR)

Dallas Cowboys owner Jerry Jones indicated Tuesday that it's likely quarterback Dak Prescott will land on injured reserve, which would knock him out of the lineup for a minimum of four games.

"It's likely we'll IR him," Jones said, "We'll see how his rehab goes."

But even before the Cowboys see how Prescott looks, they seem to be bracing for him being out for multiple weeks.

Nov 6, 2024

Saints GM Mickey Loomis: 'Pressure and stress' caused team to fire Dennis Allen

New Orleans Saints general manager Mickey Loomis defended dismissed coach Dennis Allen in his first public comments since Monday's firing.

During an interview on WWL-AM on Tuesday, Loomis blamed "circumstances" and injuries that led to a 2-7 record, pointing to quarterback Derek Carr missing games and other issues, including having 10 players on injured reserve.

"Dennis Allen, I think, is a fantastic football coach and I think anybody in our league who would talk about him thinks he's a fantastic football coach," Loomis said, via the team's official website. "He is. I think in this case the circumstances created the record. That's just the truth and a lot of people don't want to hear it."

Nov 5, 2024

Cleveland Browns trading pass rusher Za'Darius Smith to Detroit Lions

The Detroit Lions made a play to bolster their pass rush ahead of Tuesday's 4 p.m. ET trade deadline.

The Lions acquired veteran edge rusher Za'Darius Smith from the Cleveland Browns.

Detroit sent a 2025 fifth-round pick and a 2026 sixth-rounder to Cleveland in exchange for Smith and a 2026 seventh-round selection. The teams later announced the swap.

The 7-1 Lions desperately needed pass rush help after losing star Aidan Hutchinson to a broken leg in October. Outside of defensive tackle Alim McNeill, Detroit has struggled to generate consistent pressure since Hutchinson went down. In Sunday's blowout win over the Green Bay Packers, McNeill had five QB pressures, per Next Gen Stats. The rest of the defensive line had five combined.
