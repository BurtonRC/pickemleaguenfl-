Oct 23, 2024

Chiefs acquiring WR DeAndre Hopkins in trade with Titans

The Chiefs are finalizing a deal with the Tennessee Titans to acquire star receiver DeAndre Hopkins.The willingness for Tennessee to eat some of Hopkins' contract helped push the deal over the finish line for the Chiefs, who sit in the bottom quarter in terms of cap space, per Over The Cap.

Adding the five-time Pro Bowler is a massive move for the 6-0 Chiefs as they chase a third consecutive Super Bowl. K.C.'s offseason plan to revamp its receiver corps went awry. Hollywood Brown got injured before the season. Rashee Rice suffered a knee injury in Week 4. Then JuJu Smith-Schuster went down with a knee injury last week.

Adding Hopkins gives Mahomes a go-to target who can win on the outside and slots the rest of the crew into more comfortable roles. His presence allows Worthy to stretch the field and opens up the middle for Kelce.

Oct 22, 2024

RB Alvin Kamara signs 2 yr, $24.5m contract ext with Saints

Kamara and the New Orleans Saints have agreed to a two-year, $24.5 million extension which will put him under contract through the 2026 season.

The deal includes over $22 million guaranteed and the $1.5 million in incentives makes it worth up to $26 million.

The Saints will save roughly $18 million on the 2025 salary cap with Kamara's new deal in place.
