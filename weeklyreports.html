Chargers HC Jim Harbaugh gets cardiologist's results: 'The heart of an athlete'

Los Angeles Chargers head coach Jim Harbaugh was in good spirits on Wednesday just a few days after experiencing an irregular heartbeat at the start of Sunday's win over the Denver Broncos.

The reason? Harbaugh had received good news from his cardiologist just before his scheduled session with the assembled media. Such good news, it left Harbaugh boasting about his bill of health in typical Harbaughian manner.

49ers Name Curtis Robinson as 2024 Perry/Yonamine Unity Award Recipient

To celebrate the San Francisco 49ers history of exemplifying the power of teamwork to accomplish goals, the team is proud to present the 2024 Perry/Yonamine Unity Award to linebacker Curtis Robinson.

Robinson was voted by his teammates as a player who has demonstrated a commitment to promoting unity and giving back to the local community. As a leader in the locker room and for social change in the Bay Area, Robinson was selected for his outstanding skills as a unifier both on and off the field.

Virginia Halas McCaskey among 25 Hall of Fame nominees in Contributor category

Bears owner Virginia Halas McCaskey is among 25 individuals in the Contributor category eligible for induction in the Pro Football Hall of Fame as part of its Class of 2025.

Mrs. McCaskey, the only daughter of Pro Football Hall of Famer George Halas, has been the Bears' principal owner since 1983. She was born on Jan. 5, 1923, less than three years after her father founded the Bears as the Decatur Staleys and helped create what would become the NFL.
