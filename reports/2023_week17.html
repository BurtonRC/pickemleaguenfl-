That in itself should be enough to get Joe Douglas fired, but the Jets are already pledged to bring Douglas and Salah back for another year. Carolina had their best offensive performance last week against the Packers. The Bus had theirs the previous week. Coincidence? The only reason the Packers won't be in the playoffs is their defence.
